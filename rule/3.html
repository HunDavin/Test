Other booking methods

Baggage fees may different if you book via a method other than the Cambodia Airways website. Please confirm capacity and fees with your reservation provider.

Excess baggage fees

Fees are levied for any excess in total weight, regardless of the number of bags. No single bag may weigh more than 32kg. All fees are applied per flight sector. For connecting flights involving another carrier, additional fees may be levied by the other carrier. You may request a cancellation and refund of your purchased excess baggage allowance prior to check-in, as it is not refundable after check-in.
If the flight is canceled, the excess baggage allowance charges will be refunded along with the ticket fee.
To request a cancellation and refund, please contact:

Cambodia Airways Ticketing Office: +855 96 852 5555
Or send an email to: ticketing@cambodia-airways.com.

Excess baggage charge policy

dimensions

The length, width and height of any checked object cannot exceed 158 cm.

Please note:

Cambodia Airways reserves the right to deny carriage of any item deemed to be too large, or one that cannot be split / repacked. Please carry your valuables in your hand luggage.

**In any circumstances, Flammable Liquids, Chemicals or other Dangerous Goods are prohibited in checked baggage. All electronic devices that need to be stored in checked baggage must be completely switched off and stored properly before being checked in to prevent any damage during baggage handling. If you have any questions, please contact our airport ground staff. Dangerous goods include, but are not limited to: Flammable items such as gasoline, Cooking gas, matchsticks, or sulfur, Corrosive or reactive substances such as acids, alkalis, peroxides and explosives, Items that are harmful to health, such as hazardous substances, toxic substances, infectious substances and radioactive substances, Other items that pose a threat to flight, such as dry ice, liquefied nitrogen, or magnetized substances, Lithium batteries cannot be carried in checked baggage.

All information and related regulations are subject to alterations in accordance with changes in company policies. Please visit www.cambodia-airways.com for more details and the latest updates.
