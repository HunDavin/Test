Check-in at the airport counter:

Please schedule your arrival time at the airport at least 3 hours before flight departure. The Cambodia Airways check-in counters are in the Departure Hall of Phnom Penh International Airport. Our airport check-in counters in Cambodia will be open 3 hours before the flight departure. Check-in counters at airports in other countries will be open 3 hours prior to the flight departure. And all check-in counters will be closed 60 minutes prior to departure on international flights and 40 minutes prior to departure on domestic flights. Passengers who present themselves after the check-in closing time will not be permitted to board the flight and they will forfeit their seats without any refund or free transfer.

Boarding gates will be closed 20 minutes before departure.

Please take your travel documents with you.
