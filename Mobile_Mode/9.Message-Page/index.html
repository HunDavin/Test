Cambodia e-Arrival (CeA) launches for air passengers on September 1, 2024

Introducing Cambodia e-Arrival (CeA) From September 1, 2024, all air travelers entering Cambodia are required to complete an online application for a "Cambodia e-Arrival Card" within 7 days before their arrival. This e-Arrival Card will serve as a replacement for the paper Immigration Form, Health Form, and Customs Declaration by providing a single portal.
